About EDCL

At East Dallas Cricket League (EDCL), our mission is simple: Bring competitive T20 cricket closer to home. We want players in East DFW area (Garland, Rockwall, Forney, Heath, Fate etc.) to enjoy high-quality, competitive hard-tennis cricket without spending hours driving across DFW.

All skill levels are welcome - Whether you're an amateur or a seasoned club player - If you're committed to showing up every weekend, respecting your fellow players, working hard for your team, and having fun along the way, there is a place for you in our league.

You can register either as a team or as an individual player - If you register as an individual player, you will enter the EDCL Draft, where team captains select players to build balanced, competitive squads. If you want to join as a team but do not have a full squad, you can still register and enter the EDCL Draft as a team and pick players from our draft pool.

Submit your details and we'll reach out to you as soon as possible - Contact us if you have any questions!
